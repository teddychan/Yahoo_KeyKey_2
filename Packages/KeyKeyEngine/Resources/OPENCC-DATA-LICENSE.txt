OpenCC character conversion data
================================

The files in this directory:

  opencc-TSCharacters.txt  (Traditional -> Simplified, char-level)
  opencc-STCharacters.txt  (Simplified -> Traditional, char-level)

are derived from the Open Chinese Convert (OpenCC) project:

  Source: https://github.com/BYVoid/OpenCC
  Files:  data/dictionary/TSCharacters.txt
          data/dictionary/STCharacters.txt

They retain OpenCC's native format:

  <from>\t<to1> <to2> ...

(the parser in HanConvertFilter.swift takes the first target only).
